From A149 - South

Head down Redgate Hill, upon passing the 'Welcome To Hunstanton' sign take the third exit on the Oasis Way Roundabout, onto King's Lynn Road. Continue on King's Lynn Road for 4 miles.

Just upon passing the Recreation Ground on your right, turn left onto Greevegate.

Continue down Greevegate for 200 metres, turning right just after the Town Hall and before The Green.

The Hotel will be on your right, ample car parking is located directly outside with further spaces to the rear.

From A149 - North

After driving through Old Hunstanton, follow the road left onto Cromer Road.

Continue on Cromer Road for 1 mile. Upon passing the Recreation Ground on your right, turn right onto Greevegate.

Continue down Greevegate for 200 metres, turning right just after the Town Hall and before The Green.

The Hotel will be on your right, ample car parking is located directly outside with further spaces to the rear.
